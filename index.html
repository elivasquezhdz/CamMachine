CamMachine

Welcome! This project is a submission for the BitRate Machine Learning & Music Series Hackathon
This experiment uses your webcam (Please give it permission) ml5 pose detection to draw body keypoints.
Sounds will play as the bar advances trough the tiles
This is the map of the sounds:

  • rr : Ride
  • tm : Tom Mid
  • tl : Tom Low
  • sd : Snare Drum
  • kk : Kick Drum
